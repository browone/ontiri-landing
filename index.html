How it works

Four steps. About ten minutes.

1

Describe what you need to agree on

In your own words, however rough. Paste in an email, some notes, bullet points — whatever you have.

2

Ontiri drafts an agreement and flags what's missing

The AI writes a clear, readable agreement from your input — no jargon. Then it surfaces the things you probably haven't thought about yet.

3

The other person reviews and has their say

They get a simple link. No signup needed, no app to download. They review, respond, and you work through any open questions together.

4

You both confirm

When you're both happy, you lock it in. A clear record of what you agreed — that you can reference if things get fuzzy later.

The whole thing takes about ten minutes. And the other person doesn't need to sign up for anything.
